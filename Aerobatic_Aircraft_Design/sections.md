# ✈️ Aerobatic Aircraft Design

This project presents the conceptual design of a single-seat aerobatic aircraft.

---

- Type: Single-seat aerobatic aircraft  
- Configuration: Mid-wing monoplane  
- Engine: Nose-mounted piston engine  
- Landing gear: Taildragger / Tricycle (choose one)
